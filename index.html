Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna dish is a delicious, homemade meal which is ready with only a few steps and ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground pork
  • 1 lb ground beef chuck
  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 oz. pancetta
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 2 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup dry white wine
  • 28-oz. can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
