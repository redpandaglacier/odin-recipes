Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe

Description

These peanut butter cookies are the best ones yet. They have soft and chewy middles, lightly crisp edges, and a delicious peanut butter flavor. I love that the recipe is easy to make with 9 ingredients, all of which I keep on hand in my pantry. And because I have zero patience when it comes to cookies, I also love that you do not have chill the dough.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Unsalted butter
  • Brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sea salt
